PDF DOCUMENTS FOR PORTFOLIO
===========================

This folder contains your professional documents that are downloadable from your portfolio.

To add your actual PDF files, place the following files in this directory:

1. CV_Kelvin_Njagi.pdf
   - Your Curriculum Vitae/Resume
   - Should be a professional CV in PDF format

2. IBM_Cybersecurity_Certificate.pdf
   - IBM Professional Certificate in Cybersecurity
   - Export your certificate from IBM as PDF

3. IBM_AI_Certificate.pdf
   - IBM Professional Certificate in Artificial Intelligence
   - Export your certificate from IBM as PDF

INSTRUCTIONS:
=============
1. Export your CV and IBM certificates as PDF files
2. Name them exactly as shown above
3. Place them in this 'documents' folder
4. The portfolio will automatically make them downloadable

The download functionality works automatically from the portfolio interface.
Users can click on any certificate/CV card and the PDF will download to their device.

CUSTOMIZATION:
==============
If you want to add/modify documents later:
1. Edit your index.html file
2. Look for the Documents & Certificates section
3. Add or remove document cards as needed
4. Update the document file references (href attribute)

Example:
<a href="documents/YOUR_FILE_NAME.pdf" class="btn btn-compact" download>
    <i class="fas fa-download"></i> Download
</a>
